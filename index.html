Stages of Creation

Sketch

I firmly believe in sketching, this stage allows me to create the base of the design, without having to worry about colors or images. This gives a common sense of direction, and an idea of the end product.

Design

Colors mean everything, they are what drive emotion, and here is where we bring them to life. Working them into the wireframe to give the page more life and meaning.

Build

This is the meat of the project, where everything comes together, and functionality is added. At this point the site is near complete and ready for testing, which I do, heavily.

About Me

My name is Brandon Marella, I am 20 years old, and I am a web developer. I began learning to program when I was 12 years old, asking my parents for textbooks because I wanted to make video games. Little did I know that it would turn into a true passion of mine, and I would end up here.

My free time consists of learning new technologies or simply enjoying my time with friends. I also commonly brainstorm new designs or programs simply for fun. I'm a web developer for no other reason than I have a passion for it, and that passion shows in my work.

I stand by what I create and no matter the case I will make sure it is something I am proud of, for as long as it exists, that is my promise.
